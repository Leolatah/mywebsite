The Sunday Layer

 

Prep time: 25 min | Cook time: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • Olive oil ( 2tbsp )
  • Onion 
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • Bell pepper
  • Mushrooms
  • Tomato Puree
  • Tomato Paste 
  • Lean beef mince ( 500g )
  • Mozzerella 
  • Chedder
  • Basil
  • Jalepeno
  • Oregano
  • Beef Stock ( 1 cube )

White Sauce

  • Salted butter ( 2tbsp )
  • All purpose flour ( 2tbsp )
  • Milk ( 2cups; warm )
  • Pepper
  • Nutmeg

Other

  • Lasagne sheets  (10 - 13 )
  • Parmesean

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in pot, at medium heat
  2. Add diced onions,bell peppers & garlic, and saute (cook until soft)
  3. Add mince and cook till brown( add any preffered seasonings )
  4. Stir in tomato puree, tomato paste and stock
  5. Leave to simmer for 15 - 25 minutes

Preheat oven: 180

White Sauce

  1. Melt butter in a saucepan, add flour and whisk for 1minute
  2. Slowly add warm milk, while whisking, until smooth
  3. Cook until sauce thickens
  4. Add pepper and nutmeg

Asemble

  1. In a baking dish, layer: Lasagne sheets, mince, white sauce ( repeat layers,until;l slightly full )
